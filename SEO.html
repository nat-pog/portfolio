SEO Info

If you don't know, SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization, and is very important and usually overlooked when creating a website. Practices that improve SEO mainly include elements in the head of your html document. Keywords, a title, and other appropriate metadata will improve SEO. But why is SEO important? SEO will improve your website's digital reach, making it appear higher up on search engines, and makes it easier for its target audience to find.
